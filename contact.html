The Remote Experimental Team at Rosemary Hill Observatory (RETRHO) is a University of Florida organization dedicated to observing, recording, and publishing data on space phenomena. Under Dr. Jamie Tayar's guidance, the RETRHO team conducts regular observations, develops data analysis tools, and collaborates on research projects to better understand celestial events. The team provides hands-on experience for students interested in astronomy, astrophysics, and data science while contributing valuable observations to the scientific community.
